Who I Am

Hi, I'm Dominic — a Computer Science student at the National University of Singapore (NUS), specialising in Artificial Intelligence, Database Systems, and Computer Networks.

As an NUS Global Merit Scholar with a 4.83/5 GPA, I've been awarded the Dean's List three times and recognised as Top Student for Computer Network Practices (CS3103). Served as Captain of NUS Table Tennis.

I'm passionate about applying AI and machine learning to real-world problems — from surveillance systems to multimodal VLMs that outperform GPT-4.
